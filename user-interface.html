Graphical user interface (GUI) is a type of interface that allows users to interact with electronic devices through graphical icons and visual indicators such as secondary notation, as opposed to text-based interfaces, typed command labels or text navigation.

Winning Eleven 9 (Pro Evolution Soccer 5) is a football video game developed and produced by Konami and released in late 2005 (early 2006 in some regions). This year is the 10th anniversary of Winning Eleven 9. The successor of Winning Eleven 9 after 10 years is Pro Evolution Soccer 2015.

In game graphics modification, I can change the menus layout of Winning Eleven 9 with Pro Evolution Soccer 2015 menus alike layout. After some research on how the navigation screen of Winning Eleven 9 works, I finally manage to change the layout.

This can be done by changing the graphic texture in the game's system encryption named unnamed_253.bin located in e_text.afs  with the new graphic that have been edited and indexed in Adobe Photoshop.
