In association football, kit refers to the standard equipment and attire worn by players.

The terms "kit", "strip", and "uniform" are used interchangeably.

Winning Eleven series have historically lacked the sheer volume of licenses due to EA Sport' affinity to purchasing exclusive rights for their FIFA series. Most notable absences being the Premier League and Bundesliga. As such, team jerseys, names, and players may be inaccurate.

Unlike the user interface modification and advertising board modification, kit modification require the Kitserver 5 in order to modify the graphics.

Kitserver 5 is an add-on program for Pro Evolution Soccer 5, Winning Eleven 9, and Winning Eleven 9 Liveware Evolution. It is a loader and manager for various modules. Kserv Module provides the alternative way of managing players and goalkeeper kits outside of the game's .afs file.
